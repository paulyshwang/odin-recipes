Crispy Parmesan Chicken

Description

Crispy Parmesan Chicken with Garlic Herb Couscous & Lemony Roasted Carrots

Prep: 10 min | Total: 40 min | Calories: 690

Ingredients

  • 12 oz Carrots
  • 2 Scallions
  • 1 Lemon
  • ¼ Cup Panko Breadcrumbs
  • ¼ Cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 1 tsp Hot Smoked Paprika
  • 10 oz Chicken Cutlets
  • 2 TBSP Sour Cream
  • ½ Cup Israeli Couscous
  • 2 TBSP Garlic Herb Butter

Steps

  1. Prep
  2. Coat Chicken
  3. Roast Chicken & Carrots
  4. Cook Couscous
  5. Finish Carrots
  6. Serve
